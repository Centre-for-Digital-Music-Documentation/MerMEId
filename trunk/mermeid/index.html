Metadata Editor and Repository for MEI Data

MerMEId is a system for the editing and handling of music metadata, based on the the MEI (Music Encoding Initiative) XML schema. MerMEId is developed at the Danish Centre for Music Publication both for the production of thematic catalogues of works and for organizing source- and work-related information during the preparation of scholarly editions of music.

This is a preliminary project site for the MerMEId software, with a demo installation.
