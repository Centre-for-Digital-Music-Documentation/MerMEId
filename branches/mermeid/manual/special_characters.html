Special characters

The following is a list of selected unicode characters that you may copy and paste into your data. Their Unicode character codes are shown for reference. Be aware, however, that the correct displaying of these characters may depend on the fonts available on the user's computer and/or the way your data are processed for display.

Music symbols

These three accidentals are supported by a number of common fonts and are fairly safe to use. MerMEId's HTML preview furthermore supports them by providing an embedded font (see below).

Additional music symbols

  • The following music symbols are very rarely supported by Unicode fonts yet and should be used with caution. MerMEId embeds the Bravura Text music font to display them correctly in the HTML preview and below.
    If files are used in other contexts, be aware that these characters may need special treatment in order to be displayed as expected. For such purposes you may want to download the Bravura Text font. The font is freely available at www.smufl.org/fonts/. It contains all standard Unicode music characters and a lot more, though only the selection below is currently supported in MerMEId's HTML preview.
  • Older browsers often do not support printing embedded fonts. If some of these special characters are displayed correctly on screen, but do not print, you may want to try updating your browser.
